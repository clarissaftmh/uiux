Sandangku App - UIUX Case Study

Platform for Utilizing Used Clothing to Reduce Textile Waste Due to Fast Fashion Trends in Indonesia

Sandangku is an application for buying,selling, and also donating based on second-hand fashion products with the ability to personalize product purchase options according to the interests and preferences of each user. With this experience, it is expected to be able to foster higher interest in the option of purchasing second-hand fashion products rather than new products so as to reduce the fast-fashion trend and textile waste.

  • Project Type - From scratch
  • Timeline - Aug 2021 - Dec 2021
  • role - UX Designer, Researcher
  • Responsibilities - Managed Research analysis, user persona, affinity diagram, empathy maps, journey maps
